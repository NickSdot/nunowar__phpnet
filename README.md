### Rationale (300-600 words)

**PHP powers over 73% of websites on the Internet**, making it the most widely used server-side programming language in the world. A language with such impact deserves a website that truly reflects its vitality and influence. That’s why I created the concept **PHP is Awesome 🔥**.

The goal is to craft a design that's both **striking** and **memorable**, while remaining **clean**, **consistent**, and **sustainable**, a design that can **evolve** and **serve the community for years to come**. It blends modern aesthetics with [php.net](https://php.net) established tone (violet), emphasizing clarity, performance, and usability across devices.

The [documentation layout](https://nunoguerra.com/dev/php/documentation.php) is **consistent** and **straightforward**, giving developers an effortless way to find everything they need about PHP.

The idea of featuring logos of well-known companies that use PHP in the hero section is to reinforce **PHP solid reputation** while adding an aspirational touch to inspire new developers.

Let the visuals and layout, [see the full concept here](https://nunoguerra.com/dev/php/) built with Vanilla CSS and Vanilla JS, speak for themselves.

Thanks! 
**PHP is Awesome 🔥🔥🔥**
